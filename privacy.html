Privacy Policy

ProjectW products are built with privacy-aware workflows. ChargeGuard focuses on local battery diagnostics, QRcraft is designed around local scan/generated history, and RelayOTP uses encrypted relay between your own paired devices where transport is required.

Data approach

ProjectW aims to keep data local where possible and use encrypted transport only where a feature requires it.
