Problem → Solution

ProjectW apps solve specific Android problems.

Every product starts with a real inconvenience, then turns it into a clean Android workflow.

Problem

Fast charging and heat silently reduce battery lifespan.

Solution

ChargeGuard monitors charging sessions, target cutoff levels, live wattage, and thermal momentum to help users unplug at the right time.

View ChargeGuard
Problem

Most QR apps are basic, unsafe, and weak for creation.

Solution

QRcraft combines scanning, URL safety checks, QR generation, customization, history, and export-ready QR assets.

View QRcraft
Problem

OTP arrives on one phone while apps are used on another.

Solution

RelayOTP securely relays OTP codes between your own paired devices using QR pairing and encryption-first transport.

View RelayOTP

Privacy & Trust

Privacy is not a feature. It is the baseline.
