Feature architecture

Built like a product, presented like a premium launch.

ChargeGuard is built with its own feature set, technical workflow, and product story while staying connected to ProjectW.

01

Target Cutoff Protection

Arm custom charging limits at 80%, 85%, 90%, 95%, or 100% for healthier lithium-ion battery cycles.

02

Live Wattage Engine

Visualize mA flow, wattage, temperature, and charging behavior inside smooth diagnostic graphics.

03

Thermal Momentum

Detect abnormal heat rise early and warn users before throttling or battery stress gets worse.

04

TTS + Alarm System

Loud continuous alarms, torch alerts, and spoken guidance when the device reaches its selected threshold.

Architecture prepared for rooted/Magisk power users who want deeper PMIC-level control.

Track charging history, peak temperatures, average wattage, and lifetime recharge insights.

How it works

A clear technical flow users can trust.

01

Plug in

PowerConnectionReceiver detects USB, AC fast charging, or wireless charging instantly.

02

Monitor

WorkManager activates the watchdog only while charging, staying dormant when unplugged.

03

Analyze

The dashboard samples wattage, current, temperature, and trend momentum in real time.

04

Protect

At the chosen target, ChargeGuard fires alarms and voice instructions to remove power.

FAQ

Important answers without killing the premium feel.

Does ChargeGuard drain battery while unplugged?

No. The monitoring system is designed to stay dormant when the phone is not connected to power.

Is data sent to servers?

No. ChargeGuard is positioned as a local/offline utility with no trackers or commercial ads.

What makes it premium?

The app combines real diagnostics, cutoff alerts, motion-style UI, and session history instead of basic battery percentages.
