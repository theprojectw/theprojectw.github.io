Feature architecture

Built like a product, presented like a premium launch.

RelayOTP is built with its own feature set, technical workflow, and product story while staying connected to ProjectW.

01

Instant OTP Relay

Capture SMS OTP codes on the SIM phone and relay them to the paired receiver phone in real time.

02

End-to-End Encryption

Encrypt OTP payloads with AES-256-GCM and exchange keys through RSA-2048 pairing.

03

QR Pairing

Pair two phones securely by scanning a QR code with no typing or manual key sharing.

04

Auto-Fill Mode

Assist supported banking apps by filling OTP fields using Android Accessibility workflows.

05

Inbox Detection Mode

For apps that read SMS inboxes, RelayOTP can place the OTP where the banking app can detect it.

06

Foreground Reliability

Battery optimization guidance and foreground service patterns keep the listener reliable in the background.

How it works

A clear technical flow users can trust.

01

Sender phone

The phone with the SIM listens for incoming OTP messages.

02

Pair devices

The receiver phone scans a QR code and exchanges public keys.

03

Encrypt relay

The sender encrypts OTP data before transport through Firebase over TLS.

04

Receive OTP

The receiver decrypts and presents the OTP through overlay, inbox, or assisted entry.

FAQ

Important answers without killing the premium feel.

Is my OTP readable by servers?

The product is designed so OTPs are encrypted before leaving the sender phone and only the paired receiver can decrypt them.

Who is this for?

Users managing their own two-phone workflow where the SIM and banking apps are on separate devices.

Does it need internet?

Yes, both paired phones need internet for real-time relay transport.

Privacy & Responsible Use

RelayOTP Privacy & Responsible Use

RelayOTP is designed only for your own paired devices, your own SIM, and your own accounts. The app must not be used to access, forward, intercept, or control OTPs that do not belong to you.

01

Your own devices only

Use RelayOTP only between phones you own or are fully authorized to manage. Do not pair unknown, shared, stolen, or unauthorized devices.

02

Encrypted relay design

RelayOTP is designed around QR pairing, AES-256-GCM payload encryption, RSA-2048 key exchange, and short OTP retention for sensitive workflows.

03

No misuse responsibility

ProjectW will not be responsible for misuse, unauthorized OTP forwarding, account loss, banking issues, fraud, policy violations, or illegal activity caused by user behavior.
